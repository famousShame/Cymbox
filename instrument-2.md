## Instrument 2


Materials:

- Arduino Uno
- Half-Size Perma-Proto Breadboard
- Quarter Size Perma-Proto Breadboard
- 3 x LEDs
- 2 x 100uF Capacitor
- Light Sensor
- Servo Motor
- Wooden Box
- Electret Microphone Breakout Board (from Adafruit)
- 3.5mm Audio Output Jack
- 2 x Finger Cymbals
